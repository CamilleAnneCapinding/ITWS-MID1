About Doraemon

Doraemon is a blue cat robot entitled "Gadget Cat from the Future." Who helps a young boy named Nobita Nobi, who faces various challenges and problems in his life.

Doraemon's Characteristics

  1. He is gentle and caring.
  2. He has a fear of mice.
  3. He's a Time Traveler.
